Details

Coppell Student Media is the official student news website for Coppell High School in Coppell, Texas that displays the work of The Sidekick newspaper and KCBY broadcast. Its purpose is to inform, entertain and provide an educational resource for its readers. This site is a public forum for student expression, with staff members (with assistance from their adviser) making content decisions.
